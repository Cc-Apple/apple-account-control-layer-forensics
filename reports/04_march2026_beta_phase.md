# 04. March 2026 Beta-Phase Candidate

## Summary

This section documents the March 2026 beta-phase candidate.

The working interpretation is that March 2026 shows a structural density increase across restriction-layer, ID/cloud, resource-pressure, proximity/communication, and operational-trace categories.

This is not treated as proof of attribution.

It is treated as a phase-shift candidate requiring technical triage.

## Phase Model

The current working model separates the timeline into:

- Alpha-phase candidate: 2025-07 to 2026-02
- Beta-phase candidate: 2026-03 onward

The beta-phase hypothesis is based on normalized density changes and focused March 2026 event clustering.

## Normalized Density Increase

Normalized analysis showed March 2026 density increases, especially for mini1 and 15G.

Important mini1 increases included:

- Restriction-layer category: approximately 35x
- APT32-like operational-trace category: approximately 14x
- Resource-pressure category: approximately 5.5x
- APT42-like ID/cloud category: approximately 4.5x

Important 15G increases included:

- Restriction-layer category: approximately 4.4x
- Resource-pressure category: approximately 2.1x

These are treated as density changes, not simple raw file-count effects.
